FUSION MECHANISMS VISUAL COMPARISON
====================================

1. FiLMFusion (fusion_model.py)
---------------------------------
Vision [B,768] ──┐
                 │
Text [B,768] ────┼─→ MLP ─→ [gamma, beta] [B,768 each]
                 │
                 └─→ (1 + gamma) * Vision + beta
                     ↓
                 Fused [B,768]

Key: Text MODULATES vision (one-way)


2. CrossAttentionFusion (cross_attention_fusion.py)
----------------------------------------------------
Vision [B,768] ──┐
                 ├─→ Vision attends to Text ──┐
Text [B,768] ────┘                            │
                 ┌───────────────────────────┼─→ Concat [B,1536] ─→ MLP ─→ Fused [B,768]
                 │                            │
Vision [B,768] ──┼─→ Text attends to Vision ─┘
Text [B,768] ────┘

Key: BOTH modalities attend to EACH OTHER (bidirectional)
